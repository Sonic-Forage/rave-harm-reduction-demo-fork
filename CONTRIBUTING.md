# Contributing

Thank you for helping make safer dance-space education more source-backed, kind, and forkable.

## Rules

- Use nonjudgmental, noncoercive language.
- Cite public sources.
- Do not expose private scene/event info.
- Do not make medical/legal claims unless directly sourced to qualified authorities.
- Keep outreach, training, fundraising, and field operations closed until human-approved.

## Good first contributions

- Add a source-backed correction.
- Improve the glossary/checklist.
- Propose a city chapter with public resources only.
- Improve Unicode UI cards or prompt packs.
