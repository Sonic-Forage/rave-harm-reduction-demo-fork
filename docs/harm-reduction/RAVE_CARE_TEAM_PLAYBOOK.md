# Rave Care Team Playbook

A care team is a visible, nonjudgmental support layer for dance spaces. This is an educational structure, not medical authorization.

## Roles

- **Greeter / vibe witness:** notices distress early, points people to water/rest/help.
- **Chill-space steward:** keeps quiet/rest area calm and accessible.
- **Resource runner:** knows where water, exits, staff, and local resources are.
- **Documentation steward:** logs public-safe lessons after the event, never private details.
- **Approval gate keeper:** confirms what actions are closed unless a qualified human says yes.

## Before an event

- Confirm local emergency numbers and venue escalation path.
- Confirm whether naloxone/drug-checking education is legal and handled by qualified partners.
- Create signage that says “Need help? Ask early. No shame.”
- Keep private logistics out of public repos.

## During an event

- Watch for distress without surveillance vibes.
- Escalate to trained staff/emergency services when symptoms are concerning.
- Avoid giving medical advice beyond approved training.

## After an event

- Write lessons learned without names, photos, locations, or sensitive details.
- Improve the checklist and source list.
