# Care Team One-Pager

```text
╭────────────────────────────────────────────╮
│ 🫶 EXAMPLE CITY CARE TEAM                  │
├────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ 🚰 water access     🧊 chill space         │
│ 🧭 resources        🔒 closed gates        │
│ ⚕ source-backed     🪩 safer dancefloor    │
╰────────────────────────────────────────────╯
```

## Say this

“Need help? No shame. We can help find water, a quiet spot, staff, or emergency support.”

## Do this

- Notice distress early.
- Escalate concerning symptoms to trained staff/emergency services.
- Keep documentation anonymous and public-safe.

## Do not do this

- Do not diagnose.
- Do not promise safety.
- Do not post private event info.
